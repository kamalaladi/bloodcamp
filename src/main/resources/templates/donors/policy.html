Blood Donation And Donor selection

Blood Donation

  • Voluntary activity
  • Whole blood
  • Specific component

Donor Selection

  • Donors should be between 18-65 in age and over 110lbs in weight

Fit

  • Hb not less than 12g/dL
  • No measure operations in the last 6 months
  • No blood donations in past 6 months
  • No blood transfusions within the last 12 months
  • No pregnancy within the last 12 months
  • Free from severe hypertension,splenomegaly,hepatomegaly and bleeding disorders
